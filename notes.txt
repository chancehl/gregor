% Commands

/records-create-team [name]

* creates team
* asks for a list of player names
* adds players to the newly created team
* throws an error when the current user already owns a team

/records-add-player [name]

* adds a player to the current player's team
* warns when the player is already on the team

/records-remove-player [name]

* removes a player from the current player's team
* warns when the player wasn't on the team

/records-refresh

* forces a refersh the current records
